## Google Classroom integration overview

CoGuide integrates seamlessly with Google Classroom to enhance your teaching workflow without disrupting your established processes. This integration allows you to sync class data, share insights, and maintain a unified teaching experience.

<Note>
  **Seamless workflow**: CoGuide works with Google Classroom to enhance, not replace, your existing teaching tools and processes.
</Note>

## Setting up the integration

### Initial configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable integration">
    Go to CoGuide Settings > Integrations > Google Classroom and enable the integration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    Sign in to your Google account and authorize CoGuide access to Google Classroom.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select classes">
    Choose which Google Classroom classes to monitor with CoGuide.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure sync">
    Set up automatic synchronization of class data and schedules.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Permission requirements

CoGuide requires the following Google Classroom permissions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Read access">
    - **Class information**: Read class names, descriptions, and settings
    - **Student rosters**: Access student names and email addresses
    - **Class schedules**: Read class times and dates
    - **Assignment information**: Access assignment titles and due dates
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Write access">
    - **Insights sharing**: Post participation insights to Google Classroom
    - **Report distribution**: Share analytics reports with students
    - **Grade integration**: Include participation metrics in grade calculations
    - **Communication**: Send notifications about participation insights
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### Common issues and solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Authentication problems">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Cannot connect to Google Classroom
    - Authentication errors
    - Permission denied messages
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check Google account credentials
    - Verify account permissions
    - Re-authorize integration
    - Contact IT support if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Sync issues">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Data not syncing between platforms
    - Missing or incomplete data
    - Sync errors or failures
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check network connectivity
    - Verify integration settings
    - Restart sync process
    - Check for system updates
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Permission errors">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Cannot access Google Classroom features
    - Permission denied errors
    - Limited functionality
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check Google account permissions
    - Verify CoGuide access rights
    - Re-authorize integration
    - Contact administrator for help
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
