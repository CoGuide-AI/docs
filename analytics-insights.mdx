## Post-class analytics overview

After each lesson, CoGuide provides comprehensive analytics that replace guesswork with data-driven insights. These detailed reports help you understand which parts of your lesson connected best, where attention dipped, and who may need a quick follow-up.

<Note>
  **Data-driven teaching**: Use analytics to improve your teaching effectiveness and student engagement.
</Note>

### Participation heatmap

Visual representation of engagement across different topics and time periods.

#### Topic-based heatmap
- **Engagement by topic**: See which topics generated the most participation
- **Time-based analysis**: Track engagement levels throughout the class
- **Participation intensity**: Visual representation of participation density
- **Trend identification**: Spot patterns in student engagement

#### Seat-based heatmap (with seat mapping)
- **Location analysis**: See which areas of the classroom participated most
- **Individual patterns**: Track participation by seat/student
- **Group dynamics**: Understand how students interact and participate
- **Seating insights**: Data to inform future seating arrangements

### Question analysis

Detailed insights into your questioning effectiveness and student responses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Question identification">
    CoGuide automatically identifies when you ask questions during class.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Response analysis">
    Track response times, participation rates, and answer quality.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pattern recognition">
    Identify which types of questions generate the best engagement.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Effectiveness scoring">
    Rate question effectiveness based on participation and response quality.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Wait time analysis

Comprehensive tracking of response times and classroom pacing.

#### Wait time metrics
- **Average wait time**: Typical time between question and first response
- **Wait time distribution**: Range of wait times across different questions
- **Extended wait alerts**: Questions that had unusually long wait times
- **Pacing insights**: Recommendations for better question timing

#### Response patterns
- **First responder analysis**: Who typically responds first to questions
- **Response frequency**: How often different students respond
- **Question difficulty**: Correlation between wait time and question complexity
- **Engagement trends**: How wait times change throughout the class

### Overload detection

CoGuide can flag possible overload moments when answers show low confidence or accuracy.

#### Overload indicators
- **Low confidence responses**: Answers that suggest confusion or uncertainty
- **Accuracy drops**: Decreased correctness in student responses
- **Participation changes**: Sudden shifts in engagement patterns
- **Attention dips**: Moments when focus appears to decrease

#### Intervention suggestions
- **Pacing adjustments**: Recommendations to slow down or speed up
- **Clarification needs**: Suggestions for additional explanation
- **Break opportunities**: Optimal times for short breaks
- **Review moments**: When to revisit previous concepts

### Engagement highlights

Identify the most effective moments of your lesson for future reference.

#### Positive highlights
- **High participation moments**: Times when engagement was highest
- **Effective questions**: Questions that generated great responses
- **Successful activities**: Parts of the lesson that worked well
- **Student breakthroughs**: Moments of clear understanding

#### Areas for improvement
- **Low engagement periods**: Times when participation dropped
- **Ineffective questions**: Questions that didn't generate responses
- **Pacing issues**: Moments when the lesson moved too fast or slow
- **Attention challenges**: Times when focus was difficult to maintain

## Privacy and data protection

### Data handling in analytics

<Warning>
  **Privacy first**: All analytics maintain the same privacy protections as live monitoring.
</Warning>

#### What data is included
- **Participation patterns**: How often and when students participate
- **Response timing**: When students respond to questions
- **Engagement levels**: Overall participation and attention metrics
- **No personal information**: No names, faces, or identifying details

#### Data protection measures
- **Encryption**: All analytics data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access controls**: Only authorized teachers can access analytics
- **Retention policies**: Data kept only as long as needed
- **School ownership**: All data belongs to your school

### Compliance and reporting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="FERPA compliance">
    - No personally identifiable information in analytics
    - Data aggregated to protect individual privacy
    - School controls data access and sharing
    - Students cannot be individually identified
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Data minimization">
    - Only necessary data collected and analyzed
    - Analytics focus on teaching improvement, not student evaluation
    - Data used only for educational purposes
    - Regular data purging according to retention policies
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transparency">
    - Clear documentation of what data is collected
    - Easy-to-understand privacy policies
    - Regular privacy reviews and updates
    - Open communication about data use
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting analytics

### Common issues and solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Analytics not appearing">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Session not properly ended
    - Data processing still in progress
    - Privacy settings preventing access
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Ensure session was properly stopped
    - Wait for data processing to complete
    - Check privacy and access settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Incomplete data">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Technical issues during class
    - Camera or microphone problems
    - Calibration issues
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check technical logs for errors
    - Verify hardware was working properly
    - Recalibrate detection settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Export problems">
    **Possible causes:**
    - File format compatibility issues
    - Insufficient storage space
    - Permission problems
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Try different export formats
    - Free up storage space
    - Check file permissions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
